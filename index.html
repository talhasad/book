Chapters

A breif discription of vibe and mysteries you will explore by reading the various chapters

The book's opening chapter centres on a wealthy extrovert orphan girl. The tale begins on the night when the girl was high on psychedelic drugs and she starts to visualize the guy who violently raped her as a youngster, in the face of driver. So this bad trip make her panic and lashing out in rage and started a chain of events that led to the next phase of the novel.

The protagonist of the novel's second section is a middle-class introverted guy with an average appearance. But his solitude radiates an extraordinary charm. Him going out to a party is where this story starts. He encountered Lucifer at the party. " You  could have anything you desire " said by Lucifer " in exchange for my soul? " he replied. His strange wish and bizarre encounter with Lucifer alter the course of the tale.

Protagonist in the third phase of the tale deals drugs and weapons on the dark web. He is an employee of mafia boss Hiram Lodge. The story begins on a holidy night when protagonist is drinking and boss tells him to pickup his daughter from party. He accidently shot his boss's daughter under the influence of alcohol. The next thing he realises is that he will spend the rest of his life in duiwel tuis, a illegitimate prison.

This section of the story focuses on a nigger who horrifyingly abuses prisoners on behalf of an unlawful corporation. Surprisingly, he was able to keep his sinister job separate from his family, and they see him as a gentle man. Jamal's life was going well until his son was diagnosed with cancer. Soon Jamal was offered money for his son's treatment by a prisoner.

The story's final section is based on a librarian from the Alexandria Library in Egypt. He was very keen in studying about magic and superstition, and one day he discovered a book in the library that hadn't been opened in a century. That book held the secrets that would change his life forever.
